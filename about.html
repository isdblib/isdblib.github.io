About

Vision

The Bank Library's vision is to serve all users through a state of the art and friendly web portal and become part of the business processes within the IDB Group.

Mission

The Bank Library mission is to support the IDB Group business and research activities by collecting, processing and disseminating all relevant publicly available sources of knowledge and information, and to serve as the ultimate reference source in the field of Islamic economics and finance.

Quality Policy

Our Philosophy

" We want to be good at everything we do "

Our Goal

" We are committed to satisfy our customers by consistently meeting their requirements, and delight them by exceeding their expectations "

Our Approach

"We will achieve this by establishing a quality management system that defines clear quality objectives based on the ISO 9001:2008 standard and the best practices of the library profession. We will continuously review and improve the quality management system and the quality objectives to ensure they are always responding to the changing user requirements."
